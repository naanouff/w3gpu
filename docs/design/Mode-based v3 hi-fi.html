Mode-based, v2 · one job at a time

We're taking direction 04 from round one and pushing it. Eight modes in a left rail: Build · Paint · Sculpt · Logic · Animate · Light · Play · Ship. The AI lives in a floating ✦ button that opens a chat anywhere, and watches what you do — nudging you toward the right mode when it spots a mismatch. Wireframes only — structure and flow.

01 · Onboarding — first launch

zero-state · starter picker

First time a user opens w3d, the rail is visible but dimmed — they can't "pick the wrong mode". A big paper card in the center asks what they want to make. Three starters + a freeform prompt. The rail only lights up once they commit.
